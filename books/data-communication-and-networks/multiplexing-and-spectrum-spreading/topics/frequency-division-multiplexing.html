Q1. The frequency range for AM radio (530-1700 kHz) is divided into channels of 10 kHz each. If a station uses a carrier frequency of 1000 kHz, the frequency range of its signal is:

🔹 A. 995 to 1005 kHz
🔹 B. 990 to 1010 kHz
🔹 C. 1000 to 1010 kHz
🔹 D. 995 to 1000 kHz

💡 Difficulty: easy | ✅ Correct: A

Q2. A telephone company combines 12 voice channels into a group using Frequency Division Multiplexing. Each voice channel has a bandwidth of 4 kHz. The total bandwidth required for the group is:

🔹 A. 12 kHz
🔹 B. 48 kHz
🔹 C. 60 kHz
🔹 D. 72 kHz

💡 Difficulty: medium | ✅ Correct: C

Q3. In the cable TV frequency plan, the upstream data band (5-42 MHz) uses QPSK modulation with 2 bits per Hz. The maximum theoretical data rate in this band is:

🔹 B. 74 Mbps
🔹 C. 148 Mbps
🔹 D. 296 Mbps
