📝 Sample Questions

Q1. A rectangular garden is to be fenced on three sides, with the fourth side along an existing wall. If 120 m of fencing is available, what dimensions maximize the enclosed area?

🔹 A. \(30\text{ m by }40\text{ m}\)
🔹 B. \(30\text{ m by }60\text{ m}\)
🔹 C. \(40\text{ m by }60\text{ m}\)
🔹 D. \(20\text{ m by }60\text{ m}\)

💡 Difficulty: easy | ✅ Correct: B

Q2. A company’s profit function is given by \(P(x)= -2x^{2}+80x-500\), where \(x\) is the number of units sold (in hundreds). What number of units maximizes profit?

🔹 A. \(10\) hundred units
🔹 B. \(20\) hundred units
🔹 C. \(15\) hundred units
🔹 D. \(25\) hundred units

💡 Difficulty: medium | ✅ Correct: C

Q3. A cylindrical can must hold 500 cm³ of liquid. To minimize the material used, which ratio of height \(h\) to radius \(r\) should be chosen?

🔹 A. \(h=2r\)
🔹 B. \(h=r\)
🔹 C. \(h=2r^{2}\)
🔹 D. \(h= \frac{r}{2}\)

💡 Difficulty: hard | ✅ Correct: A
