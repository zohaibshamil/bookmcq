Q1. A company wants to locate a new warehouse so that the cost function \(C(x)=3x^{2}-12x+20\) is minimized. Which interval contains the x‑value where the cost is lowest?

🔹 A. \(x<0\)
🔹 B. \(0<x<2\)
🔹 C. \(2<x<4\)
🔹 D. \(x>4\)

💡 Difficulty: easy | ✅ Correct: B

Q2. A particle moves along a line with position \(s(t)=t^{3}-6t^{2}+9t\). During which time interval is its velocity increasing?

🔹 A. \(0<t<1\)
🔹 B. \(1<t<3\)
🔹 C. \(3<t<4\)
🔹 D. \(t>4\)

💡 Difficulty: medium | ✅ Correct: C

Q3. If a function's graph has a horizontal tangent at \(x=5\) and the function is decreasing on both sides of \(x=5\), what type of critical point is present?

🔹 A. Local maximum
🔹 B. Local minimum
🔹 C. Saddle point
🔹 D. No critical point

💡 Difficulty: hard | ✅ Correct: C
