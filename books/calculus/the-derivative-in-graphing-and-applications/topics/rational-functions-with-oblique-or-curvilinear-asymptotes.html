Q1. Find the oblique asymptote of \(f(x)=\frac{2x^{2}+3x-5}{x-1}\).

🔹 B. \(y=2x+3\)

💡 Difficulty: easy | ✅ Correct: A

Q2. A company models its profit by \(P(x)=\frac{4x^{2}-x+6}{x-2}\). As production \(x\) becomes very large, which linear function best approximates profit?

🔹 A. \(y=4x+7\)
🔹 B. \(y=4x-1\)
🔹 C. \(y=4x+2\)
🔹 D. \(y=4x-5\)

💡 Difficulty: medium | ✅ Correct: B

🔹 C. \(y=x^{2}+3\)
