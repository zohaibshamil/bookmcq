📝 Sample Questions

Q1. If a function \(f\) satisfies \(f'(x)=5\) for all \(x\) in \([2,10]\), which of the following statements is true about the difference \(f(10)-f(2)\)?

🔹 A. It equals 40
🔹 B. It equals 5
🔹 C. It equals 0
🔹 D. It cannot be determined without knowing \(f(2)\)

💡 Difficulty: easy | ✅ Correct: A

Q2. Suppose \(g'(x)=k\) where \(k\) is a constant. Which of the following must be true for any \(a<b\)?

🔹 A. \(g(b)-g(a)=k(b-a)\)
🔹 B. \(g(b)-g(a)=k\)
🔹 C. \(g(b)-g(a)=0\)
🔹 D. \(g(b)-g(a)=k^2(b-a)\)

💡 Difficulty: medium | ✅ Correct: A

Q3. Let \(f'(x)=k\) for all real \(x\) and \(f(2)=7\). Which of the following gives \(f(6)\) in terms of \(k\)?

🔹 A. \(7+4k\)
🔹 B. \(7+6k\)
🔹 C. \(7+2k\)
🔹 D. \(7-4k\)

💡 Difficulty: hard | ✅ Correct: A
