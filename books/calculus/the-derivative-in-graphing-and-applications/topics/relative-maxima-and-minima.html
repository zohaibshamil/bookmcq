Q1. Consider \(g(x)=x^{3}-3x\). At which \(x\)-value does \(g\) have a relative maximum?

🔹 A. -1
🔹 B. 0
🔹 C. 1
🔹 D. 2

💡 Difficulty: easy | ✅ Correct: A

Q2. Find all relative extrema of \(f(x)=x^3-3x^2+2\).

🔹 A. Relative maximum at \(x=0\), minimum at \(x=2\)
🔹 B. Relative minimum at \(x=0\), maximum at \(x=2\)
🔹 C. Maximum at \(x=1\), minimum at \(x=2\)
🔹 D. No relative extrema

💡 Difficulty: medium | ✅ Correct: C

Q3. Prove that if \(f''(x)>0\) for all \(x\) in an interval, then \(f'(x)\) is increasing on that interval. Which principle justifies this?

🔹 B. Intermediate Value Theorem
🔹 C. Fundamental Theorem of Calculus
🔹 D. Rolle’s Theorem

💡 Difficulty: hard | ✅ Correct: A
