Q1. What is the function \(F(x)\) defined by the integral \(F(x) = \int_a^x f(t) \, dt\) called?

🔹 A. A function defined by a differential equation
🔹 B. A function defined by an integral
🔹 C. A function defined by a limit
🔹 D. A function defined by a series

💡 Difficulty: easy | ✅ Correct: B

Q2. A dragster's velocity is \(v(t) = 3t + 2\) (m/s) from \(t = 0\) to \(t = 4\) s. Which integral gives the distance traveled?

🔹 A. \(\int_0^4 (3t + 2) \, dt\)
🔹 B. \(\int_0^4 (3t + 2)^2 \, dt\)
🔹 C. \(\int_0^4 (3t + 2) \, dt^2\)
🔹 D. \(\int_0^4 \sqrt{3t + 2} \, dt\)

💡 Difficulty: medium | ✅ Correct: A

Q3. Evaluate the limit \(\lim_{n \to \infty} \int_0^1 x^n \, dx\).

🔹 A. 0
🔹 B. 01-Feb
🔹 C. 1
🔹 D. Does not exist

💡 Difficulty: hard | ✅ Correct: C
