📝 Sample Questions

Q1. Which integral definition correctly defines the natural logarithm function \(\ln(x)\) for \(x > 0\)?

🔹 A. \(\int_0^x e^t \, dt\)
🔹 B. \(\int_1^x t \, dt\)
🔹 C. \(\int_1^x \frac{1}{t} \, dt\)
🔹 D. \(\int_0^x \frac{1}{1 + t} \, dt\)

💡 Difficulty: easy | ✅ Correct: C

Q2. A dragster's velocity is \(v(t) = t^2 + 3t\) (m/s). What distance does it travel from \(t = 0\) to \(t = 2\) seconds?

🔹 A. 10 m
🔹 B. 14 m
🔹 C. 18 m
🔹 D. 20 m

💡 Difficulty: medium | ✅ Correct: D

Q3. Using the integral definition of \(\ln x\), prove that \(\lim_{n \to \infty} (1 + 1/n)^n = e\). Which statement is essential?

🔹 A. \(\int_1^e \frac{1}{t} \, dt = 1\)
🔹 B. \(\int_0^1 e^t \, dt = e\)
🔹 C. \(\int_1^e \ln t \, dt = 1\)
🔹 D. \(\int_1^e \frac{1}{t} \, dt = 1\)

💡 Difficulty: hard | ✅ Correct: B
