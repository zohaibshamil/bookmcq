Q1. Which condition is necessary for a function \(f\) to have an inverse?

🔹 A. \(f\) is continuous
🔹 B. \(f\) is one-to-one
🔹 C. \(f\) is differentiable
🔹 D. \(f\) is increasing

💡 Difficulty: easy | ✅ Correct: B

Q2. If \(g(x)\) is the inverse of \(f(x)\) and \(f(4) = 8\), then \(g(8) = ?\)

🔹 A. \(4\)
🔹 B. \(8\)
🔹 C. \(\frac{1}{4}\)
🔹 D. \(\frac{1}{8}\)

💡 Difficulty: medium | ✅ Correct: A

Q3. What is the inverse of \(f(x) = \sqrt{x + 2} - 1\)?

🔹 A. \((x + 1)^2 - 2\)
🔹 B. \((x - 1)^2 - 2\)
🔹 C. \((x + 1)^2 + 2\)
🔹 D. \((x - 1)^2 + 2\)

💡 Difficulty: hard | ✅ Correct: A
