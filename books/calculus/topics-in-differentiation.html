Q1. The differential \(dy\) is related to the derivative by:

🔹 A. \(dy = dx\)
🔹 B. \(dy = f'(x)\)
🔹 C. \(dy = \frac{dx}{f'(x)}\)
🔹 D. \(\frac{dy}{dx} = f'(x)\)

💡 Difficulty: easy | ✅ Correct: D

Q2. For \(y = x^2 + 3x\), find \(dy\) when \(x = 1\) and \(dx = 0.02\).

🔹 A. \(0.2\)
🔹 B. \(0.1\)
🔹 C. \(0.01\)
🔹 D. \(0.05\)

💡 Difficulty: medium | ✅ Correct: B

Q3. Find \(dy\) for \(y = x^5\) when \(x = 2\) and \(dx = 0.01\).

🔹 A. \(0.32\)
🔹 B. \(0.8\)
🔹 C. \(0.16\)
🔹 D. \(0.04\)

💡 Difficulty: hard | ✅ Correct: B
